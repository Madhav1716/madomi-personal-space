## ğŸ¬ YouTube Mode (Free)

### How to Play Videos

1. **Add a Video**:
   - In the **Playlist** section, click **"+ Add"**
   - Enter either:
     - YouTube Video ID (e.g., `dQw4w9WgXcQ`)
     - Full YouTube URL (e.g., `https://youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ`)

2. **Play a Video**:
   - Videos appear in your playlist with numbers

3. **Sync Magic**:

   - If you join mid-video, you'll automatically catch up to the current time
   - All players stay in sync automatically

### YouTube Player Features
- Large video player in the center

- Auto-plays for everyone when someone selects it

## ğŸµ Spotify Mode (Premium Required)

### Setup (First Time Only)

1. **Login to Spotify**:
   - In a Spotify mode room, click **"Login to Spotify"**
   - You'll be redirected to Spotify's authorization page
   - Click **"Agree"** to allow the app access
   - You'll be redirected back (auto-logged in now!)

### Playing Music

1. **Add a Spotify Track**:
   - Click **"+ Add"** in the Playlist section
   - Enter either:
     - Spotify Track URL (e.g., `https://open.spotify.com/track/4uLU6hMCjMI75M1A2tKUQC`)
     - Spotify URI (e.g., `spotify:track:4uLU6hMCjMI75M1A2tKUQC`)
   - Press Enter or click **"Add"**

2. **Automatic Playback**:
   - When someone adds a track, **everyone's Spotify player** starts playing it
   - Music plays directly in your browser (not your desktop app!)
   - Beautiful album artwork, track name, and artist display

3. **Control Playback**:
   - Use the **Play/Pause button** (large green circle with â–¶/â¸)
   - When you pause, **everyone else pauses too**
   - When you resume, **everyone resumes together**

### Important Notes:
- Each person needs their own **Spotify Premium** account
- Each person needs to **login to Spotify** (one-time)
- Music plays in your **browser**, not the desktop Spotify app
- All users hear the **same track at the same time**

## ğŸ”§ Troubleshooting

### "No track selected" / Empty player?
- **Solution**: Add a song to the playlist first!

### Spotify not playing?
- **Check**: Are you logged into Spotify? (Click "Login to Spotify" if you see that button)
- **Check**: Do you have Spotify Premium? (Free accounts can't use Web Playback SDK)
- **Check**: Did someone actually add a track?

### Can't join a room?
- **Check**: Are you signed in? (Required)
- **Check**: Is the room code correct?
- **Check**: Make sure the room hasn't been deleted

### Video not playing?
- **Check**: Is it a valid YouTube video ID?
- **Check**: Is the video publicly available? (Private videos won't work)

### Chat not working?
- **Check**: Are you signed in?
- **Check**: Are you in the same room as others?
- **Try**: Refresh the page
